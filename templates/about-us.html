About Us

Coderia team is a software project team full of cooperative spirit and challenging spirit.

Coderia team was committed to developing the best user experience code learning platform and worked towards their goals throughout the 12-week project cycle. In order to facilitate learners to record when watching teaching videos, The Coderia platform supports users to upload videos online, transfer videos to text and take notes at the same time. In order to facilitate developers to contact codes while learning, the platform has a built-in code compiler to achieve both learning and practice.
